DATA QUALITY & LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

Material Intensity Data:

  - Small sample sizes use empirical distributions (bootstrap)

Capacity Projections:
  - Source: NREL Standard Scenarios
  - Years: 3-year intervals (2026, 2029, 2032, etc.)
  - Linear interpolation between data points

Model Limitations:
  - Technology mapping based on expert judgment
  - Some technologies lack intensity data (batteries, DAC, etc.)
  - No material recovery/recycling modeled
  - Assumes constant material intensity over time
