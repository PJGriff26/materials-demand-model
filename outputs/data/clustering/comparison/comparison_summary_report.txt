======================================================================
RECOMMENDATION
======================================================================

Sparse PCA is recommended as the primary clustering input because:
  1. Sparsity is ideal for HDLSS data (31 materials, 23 features)
  2. Components are directly interpretable (named: Demand Scale, etc.)
  3. L1 regularization handles multicollinearity without VIF's order-dependence
  4. See empirical metrics above to confirm or challenge this recommendation

If PCA or FA shows substantially higher silhouette/stability,
consider switching the production pipeline (main_analysis.py).
